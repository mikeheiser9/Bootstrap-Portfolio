About me

My name is Michael Heiser I was born right outside of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I graduated from Marple Newtown Senior High School in 2009. I then continued my education at Valley Forge Military Academy and College in Wayne, PA. After receiving an associates degree in humanities I transfered to University of California Santa Barbara.

After college I went to work for my family's non-profit. I was responsible for programming amoung many other things. After a few years of working for the foundation I started a clothing line called "Los Mayores." After 2 years of very hard work we decided to close the company.

I am now taking the Full stack web development and coding bootcamp at UCLA extention. I take the way to long of a drive everyday from dowtown LA where I live with my girlfriend Sophie and my dog Frodo.
